My Projects

Virtual Pet Amok

Virtual Pet Amok will help you pick up homeless animals and keep them in a warm home until adoption. You will have the ability to feed, water, play and maintain the animal's in the shelter also. If you become attached to your virtual animal, feel free to bring them home with you!

In the beginning of the program, it will provide you a list of options to take care of the different types of animals. You will have the choice to interact with Dogs, Cats, and Robot Dogs & Cats. Simply input the option you would like, and it will bring you to the next option. Your animal's stats are displayed after every choice, with their hunger, thirst and boredom are ever-increasing. On the flip side of that, their happiness, and health are decreasing. Once you feel that you've been interacting with the animal's enough, choose the last option to exit the program.
